Intro

Keeping it short until a sufficient manual is drafted.

Try get as many planets populated as you can

Populate and protect planets as fast as possible

You need a launch pad to build satellites

Satellites generate research and unlock other technologies

Moons generate as much matter as a matter factory

Farms generate colonists which can be used to populate other planets.

You can speed up the game with the double arrow icon, but watch out, asteroids will occur more quickly

Asteroids occur between every 250-1500 years depending on race

You can defend against asteroids with the turret

You can mine rhodium from the rocks in the upper left of your solar system

Everytime you build a satellite receiver, you will discover a new solar system

You can goto different solar systems by pressing the "View Galaxy" Icon
